About Cyber Troopers

Cyber Troopers is a student-driven cybersecurity club at Anurag University dedicated to creating cybersecurity awareness and developing technical skills through practical learning.

Vision
To build a community of skilled and responsible cybersecurity enthusiasts who contribute to a secure and digitally aware society.

Mission
Promote cybersecurity awareness. Provide hands-on cybersecurity learning. Encourage innovation and teamwork. Prepare students with practical cybersecurity skills.

Explore cybersecurity, ethical hacking, digital safety, CTFs, emerging technologies, workshops, awareness programs, and technical challenges through collaboration and responsible digital citizenship.
